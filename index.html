This website tracks COVID-19 data statewide and by county. Most of the State and County data for positive tests and deaths are gathered from The New York Times COVID-19 repository and The Indiana Data Hub.

Governor Holcomb’s reopening plan began on May 4th, 2020. The plan resembled the White House plan in many aspects, and both were simliar to the guidelines published by the American Enterpise Institute (AEI) and Johns Hopkins. But neither Governor Holcomb’s nor the White House’s plan included the snapback (i.e. reverting to a previous stage) triggering conditions listed in the AEI proposal. Depending on the data that’s available, I’ll also be trying to track some of the triggers in the AEI proposal and those from Governor Holcomb if any are presented.
